Eye Examinations & Contact Lens Fittings


Our dedicated doctor's thorough eye examinations for the entire family includes a prescription for spectacles and, if desired, contact lenses including soft and rigid gas permeables. If you are new to contact lenses, we will teach you how to wear and handle your contact lenses correctly and safely. In addition, your eyes will be tested for cataracts, glaucoma and ocular disorders. If you are suffering from an eye infection, dry eyes or other eye problems, a medical prescription will be provided along with follow-up care. Our doctor also co-manages with our area's distinguished ophthalmologists for your lasik, cataract and ocular needs. We are here to help address, diagnose and alleviate all your vision and ocular concerns.

Eyewear Consultations


Our experienced and licensed opticians take the time to offer expert advice in the selection of your eyeglasses from start to finish including a properly fitted frame and individually designed lenses for your specific prescription and visual needs. They carefully explain how different eyeglass frames and lens selections can impact your vision, answer any questions you might have and supply you with the most up-to-date information in order to keep your eyes healthy. Specializing in complex high prescriptions for babies to mature adults, our opticians greatest satisfaction comes from improving vision and enhancing the quality of life for your entire family.

Eyewear Adjustments and Repairs


Our licensed opticians expertly fit and adjust your eyewear for optimal vision and comfort along with recommending or making any necessary eyeglass repairs. Our goal is to build long-term relationships by providing attention to each customer's individual vision needs. We invite you to come to our store and receive the quality attention that you deserve.

Our Story.


West and Stannish Opticians is renown for showcasing the area's most distinguished selection of quality top brand eyewear combined with the latest technology in lenses for over 50 years. Our legendary customer service and exceptionally knowledgeable opticians and optometrist assist in every step of the process to achieve the best possible outcome for your eye health, vision and fabulous new look! We welcome new patients from children to mature adults starting with a thorough eye examination with our optometrist designed to match your specific vision needs followed by an impeccable eyewear consultation with our opticians. Our optical team's greatest satisfaction and pride comes from improving your vision and thereby enhancing the quality of life for your entire family. We take the time to care for you and your eyes.

We encourage you to stop by our office to experience our caring team first-hand and personalized customer service along with our vast spectacle frame and lens selection to understand why we are the longest running professional privately-owned optical business in Garden City, New York.

Meet Us

Dr. Jolie Turkmen


Optometrist

Dr. Jolie Turkmen, a graduate of the University of Maryland and SUNY College of Optometry, brings over 20 years of experience to West and Stannish. Specializing in pediatric optometry and hard-to-fit contact lenses, she is known for listening to her patients' needs and delivering tailored prescriptions for glasses and contacts. Dr. Turkmen has practiced in Maryland, New York City, and Long Island, and now offers exceptional vision care and personalized service to her patients. Outside the office, she enjoys equestrian activities, family time, and exploring new restaurants. She looks forward to welcoming both new and returning patients.

Ming Yao


Optician

With expert knowledge in frame styling and lens technology, Ming ensures each patient finds their perfect pair of glasses.

Laura Frezza


Optician

Laura Frezza, a NYS licensed optician and contact lens specialist with over 35 years of experience, is board-certified by the American Board of Opticianry. Co-founder of the Institute of Opticianry & Technology (IO Tech), an online platform for training opticians, she combines expertise, style, and the latest innovations to provide personalized care. Known for curating exceptional eyewear and contact lens options, Laura has earned multiple awards, including recognition as one of 20/20 Magazine's 50 Most Influential Women in Optical. When not at West and Stannish or traveling for IO Tech, she enjoys life in Garden City, N.Y.
