Eye Examinations


Our doctor's thorough eye examinations for the entire family, include a prescription for eyeglasses, along with testing for cataracts, glaucoma, dry eyes and eye disorders. Our optometrist takes the time to listen to her patients to address all of their visual needs and concerns. She also co-manages with our area's distinguished ophthalmologists for your lasik and cataract needs.

Contact Lens Examinations


Our optometrist examines your eyes for contact lenses including soft and rigid gas permeable lenses. She specializes in hard-to-fit and adolescent contact lenses. If you are new to contact lenses, our doctor will teach you how to handle and wear your contact lenses correctly and safely.

Eyewear Consultations


Our licensed opticians provide expert guidance in selecting eyeglasses, ensuring a precise fit and lenses tailored to your prescription and visual needs. They offer personalized advice on frames and lenses, answer your questions, and share the latest eye care information to enhance your vision and quality of life.

Eyewear Adjustments and Repairs


Our licensed opticians expertly fit and adjust your eyewear for optimal vision and comfort along with recommending or making any necessary eyeglass repairs. Our goal is to build long-term relationships by providing attention to each customer's individual vision needs. We invite you to come to our store and receive the quality attention that you deserve.

Our Team

Dr. Jolie Turkmen


Optometrist

Dr. Jolie Turkmen, a graduate of the University of Maryland and SUNY College of Optometry, brings over 20 years of experience to West and Stannish. Specializing in adolescent and pediatric optometry, she is known for listening to her patients' needs and delivering tailored prescriptions for glasses and contacts. She looks forward to welcoming both new and returning patients.

Ming Yao


Optician

Ming Yao, a NYS licensed optician, has over 20 years experience in the optical industry. Having worked in various practices in Manhattan and Long Island, Ming specializes in selecting properly fitted eyeglass frames and appropriate lenses for complex prescriptions.

Laura Frezza


Optician

Laura Frezza, a NYS licensed optician and contact lens specialist with over 35 years of experience, is board-certified by the American Board of Opticianry. Co-founder of the Institute of Opticianry & Technology (IO Tech), she blends expertise and innovation to deliver exceptional care. Recognized by 20/20 Magazine as one of the 50 Most Influential Women in Optical, Laura curates outstanding eyewear solutions.

Our Story.


For over 50 years, West & Stannish Opticians has proudly served Garden City with an unparalleled blend of distinguished eyewear, cutting-edge lens technology, and exceptional service. What began as a dream between two visionaries has grown into a legacy of care and precision.

West & Stannish was born from the shared passion of its founders, who believed in creating a place where advanced eye care meets personalized attention. West and Stannish is not just a name; it is a promise of excellence that has made us the longest-running privately owned optical business in Garden City.
